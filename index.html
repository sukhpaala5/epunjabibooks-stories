The Search Within

ਸਭ ਮਹਿ ਜੋਤਿ ਜੋਤਿ ਹੈ ਸੋਈ ॥
ਤਿਸ ਦੈ ਚਾਨਣਿ ਸਭ ਮਹਿ ਚਾਨਣੁ ਹੋਇ ॥

I am a soul in search of purpose. Gurbani teaches us that the light of the Divine is within every being, and it is through that light that everything is illuminated. I believe that instead of searching the outside world for answers, we must look deep within ourselves.

Everything we seek is already there. My writing is simply the result of what I find in those quiet moments of looking inward—capturing the reflections of the heart and turning them into words to share with the world.
